Mirrow.me

Mirrow.me is a newly released app built for fashionista’s of all sorts. This app is straightforward to use allowing you to store, and show off your wardrobe to the community within the app. By uploading your clothing styles the app will start to suggest similar style of clothing that may suit you.

Joined to development from the beginning of project. On the iOS app I mainly responsible for the network part, work with data, and their synchronization. Also I developed an API for application, back-end and admin panel
